Mango Daiquiri Recipe

This mango daiquiri is a refreshing cocktail that is perfect for a hot summer day. It is made with fresh mango, rum, lime juice, and simple syrup. Blend it up with ice for a frozen treat that is sure to cool you down.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup fresh mango, diced
  • 1/4 cup white rum
  • 1/4 cup lime juice
  • 1/4 cup simple syrup
  • 2 cups ice
  • 1 lime wedge, for garnish

Steps

  1. In a blender, combine the mango, rum, lime juice, simple syrup, and ice.
  2. Blend until smooth.
  3. Pour into a chilled glass.
  4. Garnish with a lime wedge.

Enjoy your drink!
