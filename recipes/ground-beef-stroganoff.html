Ground Beef Stroganoff Recipe

Ground beef stroganoff is a budget-friendly way to enjoy a classic comfort food. This recipe uses ground beef to make the dish quick and affordable for a family meal.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 small onion, chopped
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 1 (8 ounce) package sliced mushrooms
  • 1 (10.75 ounce) can condensed cream of mushroom soup
  • 1 cup beef broth
  • 1 tablespoon Worcestershire sauce
  • 1/4 cup sour cream
  • 1/4 cup cream cheese
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh parsley
  • 1 (16 ounce) package egg noodles

Steps

  1. In a large skillet over medium-high heat, cook the ground beef until browned. Drain off excess fat, and set aside.
  2. Add the onion, garlic, and mushrooms to the skillet. Cook until the onions are soft and the mushrooms are browned.
  3. Stir in the cream of mushroom soup, beef broth, and Worcestershire sauce. Bring to a simmer, and cook for 5 minutes.
  4. Stir in the sour cream and cream cheese until well combined. Add the cooked ground beef back to the skillet, and cook until heated through.
  5. Meanwhile, bring a large pot of salted water to a boil. Add the egg noodles, and cook according to package directions. Drain well.
  6. Serve the stroganoff over the cooked egg noodles, and garnish with chopped parsley.

Enjoy your meal!
